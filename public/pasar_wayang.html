PASAR WAYANG

The brief was to create an interactive space at the Singapore River that engages the public through narrative. The site I chose was the edge of the river near the pedestrian bridge. My story was imagined around a child’s perspective—where storytelling booths, food stalls, and hidden walkways come alive. My villager was fictional and she was a shy schoolgirl by day and a bold storyteller by night.
